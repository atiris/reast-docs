# Reast Platform

Reast (rea.st) is a web platform for reading and creating interactive stories written in the [Rea language](/spec/). It's the hosted service built on top of the [Reast Engine](/engine/) — the platform adds accounts, publishing, a catalog, cooperative reading infrastructure, and a built-in editor around what the engine alone only renders.

At a glance, the platform lets a reader browse a public catalog and read stories in-browser, with or without an account, and lets an author write a story (in the built-in editor or any text editor), attach media and metadata, and publish it at one of three visibility tiers — `public` (listed in the catalog), `unlisted` (reachable only by direct link), or `private` (visible only to the author) — into personal shelves and reading groups that both readers and authors use to organize and share stories.

Every story an author publishes through the platform — whether written in the built-in editor or uploaded from a text editor — is stored and served as a `.reast` package; see the engine's [package format reference](/engine/package-format) for what's inside one.

## For Readers

- **Browse stories** — Discover interactive narratives by genre, language, or popularity
- **Read anywhere** — Works in any browser, on any device, even offline
- **Make choices** — Your decisions shape the story's outcome
- **Track progress** — Reading position is saved automatically
- **Personal library** — Bookmark favourites, take notes, rate stories
- **Groups** — Join reader communities around shared interests

## For Authors

- **Write in Rea** — Simple markup language for branching narratives
- **Built-in editor** — Write, preview, and test stories directly in the browser
- **Builder tool** — Visual story structure editor for complex narratives
- **Publish instantly** — Share your work with the community
- **Author page** — Customisable profile showcasing your published works
- **Analytics** — See how readers engage with your stories

## Platform Features

- **No login required for reading** — Browse and read public stories without creating an account
- **Full offline support** — Downloaded stories work without internet
- **Multiple languages** — Interface available in 30+ languages
- **Accessibility** — Keyboard navigation, screen reader support, high-contrast themes
- **Privacy-first** — Minimal data collection, transparent policies
